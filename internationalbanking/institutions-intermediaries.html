Expert banking solutions for Intermediaries

If you’re looking for dedicated international business expertise and banking services with a truly global reach, Barclays can help you.

We specialise in helping Intermediaries build and protect their wealth. And we have the products, services and expertise to help your company achieve its financial goals.

Take advantage of a world of opportunities

Backed by the Barclays Group, our international network covers more than 60 countries around the world. With teams based in key financial jurisdictions - from the Channel Islands to Switzerland, Cyprus and Gibraltar - we can facilitate your international trading and help you make the most of business opportunities overseas.

Enjoy a dedicated and highly personalised service

You’ll receive expert support from your dedicated relationship team, who will be specialists in your market sector. They will work closely with you to understand your specific requirements, help you select the right products and services and connect you to the wider range of financial support and expertise available from across the Barclays Group.

Learn more about how Barclays can help Intermediaries

  • Fiduciary services
    We can assist you with all aspects of managing wealth through Barclays Advisory, our specialist service for fiduciary, estate planning and wealth advisory companies.
  • Professional financial advisers
     Barclays is ideally placed to help you manage your clients' wealth. We can offer your clients a single relationship across wealth management and banking and access to our full range of products and services.
  • Offshore financial markets
     If you're in the captive insurance, international life insurance, banking or fund management sector, we can offer you bespoke banking products and services specifically tailored to your requirements.
  • International trading clients
     We can help you streamline your international trading and take advantage of opportunities around the world.
  • Financial intermediaries
    With over 500 intermediary clients in offshore locations and over 50,000 intermediary managed accounts, Barclays is one of the leading providers of banking services to this sector. Discover how we can help you.
  • Charities
     As one of the UK's leading charity investment management experts, we will work with you to help you meet your charity's income and capital objectives.
  • Brokerage services
     Whether you're looking for brokerage services for your employees, shareholders or clients, we have the specialist expertise and solutions you need.
