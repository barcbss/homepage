    Careers

    At Barclays we truly understand that our success is dependent on the performance of our people and this underpins how we hire, develop and retain the talented individuals who will enable us to grow and evolve the business and achieve our ultimate goal of becoming the premier global wealth manager and adviser.

    We are a top-ten global player, with global reach in a fast growing industry and employ high quality talented individuals and help them to achieve their career goals by providing them with a stimulating working environment.

    We have a number of opportunities throughout Barclays at any given time:

    Graduates

    We believe we have the best international graduate training programme that the Wealth Management industry has to offer. Whether you're about to graduate with a degree, Masters or MBA, or are looking for internship opportunities, Barclays offers a unique opportunity to make your mark in this fast-moving industry.

    Internships

    We run a 10-week intern programme over the summer, as well as a one-week spring session for university students, providing a unique insight into how the wealth management industry works for students considering a career in private banking.

    Embark - New Private Bankers

    Embark is a programme designed to offer individuals based in the UK and Ireland with a new career in private banking. We recognise that there are many transferable skills gained from various disciplines and Embark offers individuals with an opportunity to employ the breadth of their experience whilst carving out a new career as a Private Banker. To find out more about the Barclays Embark programme and how to apply, please visit the Embark section.

    Experienced Professionals

    All our current vacancies are advertised on the Barclays Careers website. You are able to search for roles by location, business area and/or role type. Once you have found a suitable role please do apply online and ensure that you attach your CV.

    Barclays in Scotland

    In addition to the opportunities above, Barclays are currently recruiting for a number of vacancies in our Operations centre of excellence in Glasgow. Opportunities exist for both experienced professionals and graduate-calibre fresh talent.

    Two Ticks scheme

    As a member of the Two Ticks scheme, we are proud to show our commitment to employing disabled people and judging people on their abilities alone.
