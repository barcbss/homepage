Private Bank

The Barclays Group has been managing the wealth of individuals and families for over 300 years. At Barclays we have Private Bankers dedicated to managing the wealth of clients in over 50 countries worldwide.

Personal service

To deliver a superior level of service, we match you with your own dedicated Private Banker. Their role is to anticipate and understand your needs and to help you achieve your immediate and long-term wealth goals. We never underestimate the importance of personal chemistry.

Access to market-leading investment advice

We deliver objective advice sourcing the best solutions from the entire market. Our relationship with Investment Banking, a leading investment bank, gives our clients access to market-leading investment advice often reserved for institutional clients.

Our appreciation that every investor is different has led us to develop an innovative approach that we call our Investment Philosophy.

UK and Ireland

For individuals living in the UK and Ireland we offer bespoke banking and investment solutions to clients with over £500,000 of investible assets. We also provide a comprehensive range of investment advice and products across all asset classes, wealth structuring, lending and banking services.

UK resident non-domiciled clients

If you live in the UK or are about to move to the UK and have your roots elsewhere, we can help you make informed decisions about your financial future based on your personal, family and business goals.

International Private Banking

Our International Private Banking business operates in both onshore and offshore markets. We have the international expertise and knowledge that comes from many years of offering bespoke banking and investment solutions to clients from diverse backgrounds.
